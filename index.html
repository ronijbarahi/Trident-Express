04 — About

A logistics partner,
not a vendor.

Trident is a subcontracted last-mile operator built for the modern enterprise — designed around reliability, accountability, and a quietly relentless improvement loop.

We started moving freight for two retailers in 2024. Today we run a vetted driver network and a growing EV fleet across the Greater Melbourne grid — quietly, dependably, on time.

Every Trident driver is trained, insured, and uniformed. Every drop is captured, time-stamped, and reconciled. No surprises, no hidden fees, no missing parcels.
